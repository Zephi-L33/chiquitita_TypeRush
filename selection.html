Tutorial!

To play Typerush, you first need to select a difficulty: Easy, Medium, Menacing, or Endless. Each level increases speed and difficulty of words!

Press the "start" button to begin. A timer and words will appear, you must type them exactly as shown and within the time limit.

Each difficulty will have a required point goal. You are to meet the score before the time runs out to WIN !

For Endless: No time limit, Keep trying as long as you can! Measure your finger's skills.
